Product Tips

Soil Mix

  • Use our mix straight from the bag — no amendments needed
  • 50% inorganic grit for fast drainage
  • Re-pot cacti every 2–3 years

Fertiliser

  • Apply during the growing season (Mar–Sep)
  • Do not fertilise in winter — plants are dormant
  • One application lasts up to 3 months

Gardening Terms

Perlite
A volcanic glass material that is expanded by heat into lightweight white granules. Widely used to improve drainage and aeration in potting mixes.
Slow-Release Fertiliser
A type of fertiliser coated to gradually release nutrients over weeks or months, reducing the risk of over-fertilising and root burn.
pH (Potential of Hydrogen)
A scale measuring the acidity or alkalinity of soil. Cacti and succulents generally prefer a slightly acidic to neutral pH of 6.0–7.0.
Propagation
The process of creating new plants from an existing one — through offsets, leaf cuttings, or seeds — without purchasing new stock.
